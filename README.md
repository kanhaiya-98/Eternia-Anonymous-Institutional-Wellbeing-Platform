## 📖 The Story That Made Us Build This

Imagine a second-year engineering student. It's 2 AM. The pressure of exams, a family crisis, and the loneliness of a hostel room have converged into something unbearable. They want to talk to someone — anyone. But:

- The college counsellor only takes appointments during office hours.
- Their friends don't know they're struggling; they're afraid of being judged.
- Calling a helpline feels too formal, too permanent, too scary.
- There is no one. There is nothing. There is only silence.

Now multiply that moment by **13,892** — the number of students who took their own lives in India in 2023 alone. That's **38 students every single day**. One every 38 minutes.

We refused to accept that this was inevitable.

**Eternia was built for that student at 2 AM.** It's a platform where help is available before it becomes a crisis — where anonymity removes the stigma, where a voice in the dark can find another voice without giving up their name or their dignity.

This is not a form to fill out. This is not a helpline to call. This is a living, breathing support ecosystem — embedded directly inside the institution that the student already belongs to.

## 🚨 The Crisis That Demands Action

These are not abstract statistics. These are India's students.

| Year | Student Suicides (NCRB) | Change |
|---|---|---|
| 2013 | 8,423 | — |
| 2019 | 10,335 | +22.7% from 2013 |
| 2022 | 13,044 | +26.2% from 2019 |
| **2023** | **13,892** | **📈 All-time record high** |

**Key data points from NCRB 2023:**
- Student suicides now constitute **8.1% of all suicides** in India — up from 6.2% a decade ago.
- The 15–29 age group collectively accounts for a disproportionate share of total national suicides.
- **Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu** account for the highest numbers — states with the largest and most competitive educational ecosystems.
- The crisis is accelerating: a **65% rise over a single decade**, outpacing the overall national suicide increase of 23.2%.

**The root causes are not mysterious.** Academic pressure, financial stress, social isolation, the stigma of seeking help, and — critically — **the absence of accessible, anonymous, always-on mental health support within institutions.**

Eternia directly addresses the last of these. And it is the one that technology can solve right now.

## 🏛️ Government Mandates & Regulatory Alignment

Eternia is not just a good idea — it is the answer to a growing legal and policy mandate from the Government of India, CBSE, UGC, and the Supreme Court.

### 📋 CBSE Circulars

**Circular No. TRG-03/2026 (February 20, 2026) — Mental Health & Wellness and Social Emotional Learning**
> CBSE mandated that all teaching and non-teaching staff undergo **mandatory mental health training at least twice a year** by certified professionals. Schools must establish regular awareness programmes for students and parents. This was issued in response to a Supreme Court judgment (Criminal Appeal No. 3177/2025, July 25, 2025) which laid down guidelines that all CBSE-affiliated schools are required to comply with.

**CBSE Circular — Enhancing Counselling & Wellbeing Services (June 26, 2024)**
> Under CBSE Affiliation Bye-Laws Clause 2.4.12, every Secondary and Senior Secondary school must appoint a full-time **Counselor & Wellness Teacher** — a qualified psychology graduate or equivalent. CBSE launched a nationwide mapping exercise to audit counselling infrastructure across affiliated institutions. Eternia operationalizes this mandate digitally: our Doctor and SPOC dashboards are the digital backbone that these counsellors need to work effectively.

**CBSE Mental Health Conclave (September–October 2024)**
> In alignment with **NEP 2020's holistic development vision**, CBSE organized a National Counselors' Conclave specifically titled *"Shaping the Landscape of Well-Being in Schools"* — confirming that mental health support in educational institutions is now a national policy priority, not an optional add-on.

---

### 🎓 UGC Guidelines — Higher Education Institutions

**UGC Draft Guidelines: Uniform Policy on Mental Health & Well-Being for Higher Educational Institutions (2025–2026)**
> The University Grants Commission published draft guidelines calling on all HEIs to:
> - Establish a **dedicated Mental Health & Well-being Centre** with private spaces for appointments.
> - Maintain **anonymized, coded records** of counselling sessions with strict data retention and destruction policies.
> - Monitor outcomes through the **MANASSETU portal** — tracking whether interventions actually reduce attrition and loss of life.
> - Collaborate with NRF, ICMR, ICSSR, and WHO to build evidence-based interventions.

**Eternia directly implements every point of this framework:**
- The **BlackBox** and **Peer Connect** features provide the "safe, private space."
- Our `user_private` table uses `_encrypted` fields with coded storage — matching the UGC's anonymized records mandate.
- The **escalation system** (Doctor → SPOC) is the digital embodiment of the UGC's "referral process" requirement.

**UGC Guidelines for Promotion of Physical Fitness, Sports, Students' Health, Welfare, Psychological and Emotional Well-Being at Higher Educational Institutions (2023)**
> This foundational UGC document established that psychological and emotional wellbeing is a core institutional responsibility — not a student's individual problem to manage alone.

---

### ⚖️ Supreme Court of India

**Sukdeb Saha v. State of Andhra Pradesh (2024)**
> The Supreme Court ruled that the State carries a **positive constitutional obligation** under Articles 21 and 21A to ensure the mental wellbeing of students in educational and workplace environments. The Court condemned the absence of a coherent national framework for student suicide prevention and directed states to treat student suicide as a consequence of **policy failure**, not private tragedy.

**Supreme Court Judgment — Criminal Appeal No. 3177/2025 (July 25, 2025)**
> The Court issued comprehensive guidelines on student mental health, mandating institutional compliance across all CBSE-affiliated schools. CBSE Circular TRG-03/2026 was issued directly in response.

**Eternia's escalation system — where a doctor can escalate a critical case to a SPOC with emergency contact details in under one second — is a direct technical implementation of the constitutional duty the Supreme Court identified.**

---

### 🔐 Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

India's landmark data privacy legislation has **direct and explicit implications** for mental health platforms operating within educational institutions.

| DPDP Requirement | Eternia's Implementation |
|---|---|
| Informed consent for data collection | Eternia Code-based onboarding with explicit role-based consent |
| Data minimization — collect only what's necessary | `user_private` table is separate; PII stored only where required |
| Anonymization of sensitive records | Fake email pattern (`username@eternia.app`) — real identity never exposed to platform |
| Right to withdraw consent | Session-based architecture; students can leave at any time |
| Protection of minors' data (under 18) | Institution-gated access via Eternia Code — no public signup |
| Data breach notification obligations | Architecture uses Supabase RLS; service role key is server-side only, never client-exposed |
| Anonymized storage of mental health records | AES-256-GCM encryption flagged on all `_encrypted` fields in `user_private` |

> The DPDP Act recognizes that mental health data carries uniquely high privacy risk — comparable to special category data under GDPR. Stigma, discrimination, and social harm are documented consequences of mental health data breaches in India. Eternia was architected from day one with this reality in mind.

## 📸 Platform Overview

Eternia is a full-stack web application that connects students with mental health support while preserving anonymity at every layer. Institutional authorities — admins, SPOCs (Single Point of Contact officers), and doctors — have dedicated real-time dashboards powered by live database subscriptions.

```
Student signs up with their institution's Eternia Code
            ↓
Role-based dashboard assigned (Student / Doctor / SPOC / Admin / Intern)
            ↓
Student can: Book doctor session · Join BlackBox voice · Connect with peers
            ↓
Doctor can: View student sessions · Generate ETR codes · Escalate critical cases
            ↓
SPOC receives: Live escalation alerts · Emergency contact details · Acknowledge & close
            ↓
Admin manages: Full user roster · Role assignments · Complete escalation audit log
```

## ✨ Feature Breakdown

### 🔐 Authentication & Identity — The Eternia Code

The **Eternia Code** is Eternia's central identity primitive. An institution issues a code (e.g., `DEMO2025`). Students onboard by entering or scanning that code — no email required, no public registration. This ensures:
- Only verified institutional members access the platform.
- Student identity within the platform is separate from their real-world identity.
- The institution retains control of who is in the system.

**Technical implementation:**
- Username + Password authentication (no email ever shown to user).
- Fake email pattern: `{username}@eternia.app` stored in Supabase Auth under the hood.
- Session cookies managed by `@supabase/ssr` with automatic refresh.
- Role-based routing after login → Student, Doctor, SPOC, Admin, Intern.

---

### 🎙️ BlackBox — Anonymous Voice Room

**The most powerful feature. A voice room where identity doesn't exist.**

BlackBox is a real-time, anonymous voice channel powered by **Agora RTC SDK (WebRTC)**. Students join using a random UID — no names, no profiles, no history. Just voices in a shared, safe space.

- Animated audio-reactive canvas orb — the room feels alive.
- Mute / unmute controls.
- Multiple simultaneous users in the same channel.
- Zero identity exchange. Truly, structurally anonymous.

> BlackBox is the digital equivalent of speaking in the dark with someone who understands — the kind of conversation that sometimes saves a life.

---

### 👤 Student Portal

The student's home base — designed to feel welcoming, not clinical.

- **Book Doctor Sessions** — structured counsellor access with scheduling.
- **Peer Connect** — join voice channels with peers who've opted in to connect.
- **BlackBox** — anonymous open voice room (described above).
- **Profile & Emergency Contact** — students can privately store emergency contact details, accessible only to SPOCs during an escalation. Never visible to peers.

---

### 🩺 Doctor Dashboard — Escalation Engine

Doctors (counsellors/therapists) use this to manage their student interactions and — critically — to escalate cases that need immediate institutional intervention.

**The Escalation Flow:**

```
Doctor clicks "Escalate Case" for a student
        │
        ├── API fetches student's emergency contact from user_private (encrypted)
        ├── Inserts escalation record into escalation_log table
        └── Broadcasts to "escalations" Supabase Realtime channel
                │
                ├── SPOC Dashboard → escalation card appears in < 1 second
                └── Admin Dashboard → appears in Escalations audit tab
```

This is not a form that sits in an inbox. This is a **live alarm** that reaches the right person instantly.

---

### 🛡️ SPOC Dashboard — Real-Time Crisis Response

The SPOC (Single Point of Contact) is the institutional officer responsible for emergency response. Their dashboard is built for **speed under pressure**.

- Live subscription to escalation events — sub-1-second latency via Supabase Realtime.
- Each escalation card shows: Student username · Eternia Code · Escalation level (L1/L2/L3) · Emergency contact name, relation, and **clickable phone number**.
- **Acknowledge & Close** — removes the case from the active queue and persists to DB. Acknowledged cases never reappear after refresh.

---

### 🔧 Admin Dashboard — Institutional Command Center

- Live user list from Supabase — no mock data, ever.
- Role count breakdown: Students / Experts / SPOCs / Interns / Admins.
- **Add User modal** — create Doctor, SPOC, Intern, or Admin accounts directly.
- Full escalation history tab — complete audit log for compliance reporting.
- Real-time escalation feed (same Realtime channel as SPOC).

## 🔒 Privacy & Security Architecture

Privacy is not a feature in Eternia. It is the **foundation**.

- **Structural anonymity** in BlackBox — Agora RTC random UIDs, no identity exchange possible at the protocol level.
- **Fake email abstraction** — `username@eternia.app` pattern means students' real emails never enter the system.
- **Encrypted PII** — All fields in `user_private` are labelled `_encrypted` (AES-256-GCM in production; stored plain in hackathon prototype for demo purposes).
- **Service role key is server-side only** — the most privileged credential never touches the browser.
- **Row Level Security (RLS)** enabled on all core tables — users can only access their own data by default.
- **Middleware-protected routes** — all `/dashboard/*` paths require valid authenticated sessions.
- **Session management via `@supabase/ssr`** — automatic cookie refresh, no manual token handling.

*Full production deployment requires a comprehensive security audit and DPDP Act 2023 compliance review before handling real student data.*

## 🗄️ Database Setup

Run these SQL migrations **in order** in your Supabase SQL Editor:

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `001_schema.sql` | All 17 core tables — users, institutions, sessions, escalations, etc. |
| `002_rls_policies.sql` | Row Level Security policies for all tables |
| `003_triggers.sql` | `handle_new_user` — auto-creates `public.users` record on Supabase Auth signup |
| `004_seed.sql` | Demo institution + Eternia Code `DEMO2025` |
| `005_fix_handle_new_user.sql` | Makes trigger resilient — audit log failure no longer blocks signup |
| `006_escalation_log.sql` | Escalation log table with emergency contact columns |

**Critical Supabase configuration:**

1. **Disable Email Confirmation:**
   Dashboard → Authentication → Providers → Email → **Toggle OFF "Confirm email"**

2. **Agora Testing Mode:**
   [console.agora.io](https://console.agora.io) → Your Project → Ensure App Certificate is **not enabled** (Testing Mode allows `null` tokens for BlackBox)
